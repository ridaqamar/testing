Quotes from the Book:

“I invite everyone to discover for themselves the historical, factual, dated account of humanity’s beginning years. I have not gone beyond to impute anything other than that which is clearly and specifically written, keeping closely to the directly disclosed, or revealed. “This work is not a theological work; neither is it an evangelical work. It is simply a process of simple, deductive reasoning with mathematical calculation of dates through careful application of numbers as presented in the written text of scriptures.”

Human Genealogy and Human flagship trace the unbroken chain of descent from Adam, the first man to Jacob, the first 22 generations of human existence on earth, over a period of 2,255 years, counting from Day 6. Also, the book, relying on the science of numbers in the form of simple addition and subtraction, dates:

  • The year of Enoch’s translation.
  • The year of the destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah.
  • The year of Noah's flood.
  • The 7 years of plenty and the 7 years of famine during the reign of Joseph under the Pharaoh of Egypt.

The author applied the analytical skills of a lawyer, under inspiration of the Holy Spirit, to unravel in clear details, the human family tree in a direct, unbroken line from Adam, the first man, to Jacob, father of the Jewish nation.

Also, discussed within certain specific time frames in the book are notable events such as,

  • Tower Of Babel.
  • Division Of the Earth.
  • Noah's Flood.
